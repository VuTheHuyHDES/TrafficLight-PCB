Key Design Highlights

ESD & Surge Protection

TVS diodes on all external sensor/communication lines. Spark gaps and MOV components on mains supply inputs. ESD protection up to 15kV.

IEC 61000-4-2

High Efficiency Power

Synchronous buck regulators (efficiency > 92%) on Master and Driver boards. Minimal thermal footprint under continuous 24/7 operating conditions.

DCDC Switching

Design for Assembly (DFA)

Single-sided component placement on all peripheral boards to minimize stencil costs. Common footprint sizing (0805, SOIC, QFN) for optimized pick-and-place.

IPC-7351

Design for Testability (DFT)

Dedicated copper test points on all critical power rails, clock signals, and UART channels. Sized and spaced for mechanical JIG bed-of-nails testing.

JIG Test Points

Signal Integrity (SI)

Differential pairs routed with controlled 90-ohm impedance for communication channels. Ground guard rings surrounding critical oscillator traces.

Controlled Zo

Thermal Management

Via farms and exposed copper pads under switching regulators and LED drivers. Optimized thermal paths keeping components under 55°C at peak loads.

ΔT < 15°C
